Music

My main hobby for the past few years has been writing and performing music. I was heavily involved in the Music Players' Society at university, being elected first as Rock Band Coordinator and then as President in my second and third year. It taught me a lot about working with people in a more diverse team than my course, taking responsibility for fixing problems and organising events which benefit the society of 150+ members.

Jumper Boy

I'm also a guitarist/vocalist and songwriter for the band Jumper Boy - A 4 Piece Indie Rock band with Math Rock, Post Punk and Lo-Fi influences. We've released a 5-song EP online and played increasingly popular gigs around the UK.
