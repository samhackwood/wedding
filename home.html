Our story

08.10.2017 - We Met at a Flat Party

We first met at a party at Anaïs' university accommodation. Sam lived on the ground floor and Anaïs lived on the 7th floor. A mutual friend suggested inviting Sam (while he was already in bed) and he turned up to the party in his pyjamas with a bowl of lasagne!

13.10.2017 - Our First Date

We had coffee in a cafe in Bloomsbury and then caught a bus to Westminster. We sat on the stairs at Waterloo station and listend to music while waiting for Anaïs' train to Chessington.

24.09.2019 - We Moved in Together

After a long and very stressful period of flat-hunting, we finally moved into our first home together. We lived in Stratford with our friend Maisy, overlooking the Olympic Park and the West Ham stadium.

22.10.2020 - We Got Magic

After Anaïs got a job offer, we made the snap (and arguably) rash decision to get a kitten that night. Magic was only 4 weeks old and spent his first weeks following Sam around our flat. They have been inseperable ever since!

21.06.2021 - We Got Yury

With lockdown lifting and Sam spending more and more time out of the house, we decided to get a friend for Magic. Magic and Yury got off to a rocky start but now they are best pals. It feels strange to think there was ever a time when they weren't a duo.

19.07.2023 - We Got Engaged

It may not have been the romantic gesture Sam had in mind, but on 19.07.23 we got engaged. It was a very sweet and intimate moment, shared only with our cats. We celebrated with a cup of tea.
