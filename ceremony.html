Ceremony

(The City Rooms)

The ceremony and reception will be held at the City Rooms, Leicester.

Schedule

If you are joining us during the day, please make sure to get to the venue by 1pm. The venue will be open for guests to arrive from 12:30pm.

If you are joining us for the evening, please come to the venue at 6:30pm. We will provide further information about evening events closer to the time.

Dress Code

The dress code is black tie. For the men, this is a dinner jacket with a black bow tie, and for the ladies, this is a nice evening gown or a cocktail dress. We want everyone to feel comfortable, so while we're aiming for a polished look, we understand that kids might not be too keen on formal wear. So, feel free to keep it a bit more relaxed for the little ones. Your presence means the world to us, and we look forward to sharing this special day with you!

Directions

The City Rooms is at 16 Hotel Street, Leicester, LE1 5AW. It is a short walk or bus ride away from Leicester train station and there are several large carparks nearby. You can get a discounted parking rate at the NCP Rutland Centre carpark. If you decide to park at the NCP Rutland Centre carpark, please show your parking ticket to the venue staff when you arrive and they will tell you how to access the discount.
